# Northstar Operations Brand Brief

- Product: enterprise operations console for regulated logistics teams.
- Personality: calm, precise, trustworthy, modern, not playful.
- Primary brand color: #005BAC.
- Supporting teal: #008C95.
- Neutral background: #F4F7FA.
- Use system fonts when licensed brand fonts are unavailable.
- Avoid gradients, glassmorphism, excessive rounding, and decorative illustrations.
- Required languages: Simplified Chinese and English.
