# Budget Audit AI — Product Requirements

Users upload budget documents, run rule-based and AI-assisted checks, review findings, assign remediation owners, and export an audit report.

Required screens:
1. Overview dashboard.
2. Audit task list with filters and bulk actions.
3. Finding detail with source evidence and remediation timeline.
4. Empty, loading, validation error, permission-restricted, and destructive-confirmation states.

The product is for Chinese government and enterprise finance teams. It should feel rigorous, legible, and efficient.
